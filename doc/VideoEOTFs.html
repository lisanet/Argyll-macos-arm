"Pure Gamma" curves

They don't exist, unless your display has a perfect, zero black. But even if it does have a perfect black, the black as seen by your eye won't be perfect unless you are in a pitch black room and the display is all black, because any stray light in the room or from other non-black elements on the display will dilute the perfect black due to glare.
